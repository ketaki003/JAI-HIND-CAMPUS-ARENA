Project Green Tee, is a quest born from the fraught uncertainties bestowed by the pandemic, to impart opportunities for alternative employment, financial independence, and personal empowerment, to women trudging in fragile hope through the landscape of some parts of Mumbai’s.
This initiative served two purpose – humanity and sustainability . It featured the participation of this marginalized community to recycle single-use plastic bottles into hand-sewn, stylish recycled t-shirt garments fit for everyday use.
We tackled a mass scale outreach effort to obtain plastic bottles from personal sources to external ones the likes of popular hotel chains. These bottles were then further segregated according to color, with their caps and labels removed, and shredded into small colorful chips. Following a warm rinse and trip to the ovens, the flakes were melted into tiny pellets, undergoing stringent filtration and contamination removal. The filaments are combined and winded into stronger threads to create weighted spools of yarn. The yarn is subsequently stretched, carded, and roved before it is spun into fabric and is sent to our beneficiaries workplace and then the fabric is sewn into our Green – Tees by our beneficiaries . And this is how 7 PET bottles can be transformed into 1 – uber comfortable, stylish t – shirt. Our team of over 120 volunteers were trained to promote sales within their networks.We reached out to fashion influencers and thrift stores for brand visibility.GreenTee has also garnered a substantial instagram presence of 800+ followers upon its launch.We have impacted an audience of 900+ people through seminars and upskilling workshops on sustainable practices, designed to draw attention to our project.

About **SPICMACAY (Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music and Culture Amongst Youth)** is a non-political, nationwide, voluntary movement founded in 1977 by Dr. Kiran Seth, Professor-Emeritus at IIT, Delhi who was awarded the ‘Padma Shri’ for his contribution to the arts in 2009. SPICMACAY’s intention is to enrich the quality of formal education by increasing awareness about different aspects of Indian heritage and inspiring the young mind to imbibe the values embedded in it. It is an apolitical, non-profit, voluntary movement, committed to increasing awareness of Indian culture & classical art forms, Committed to Preserving & promoting India’s intangible heritage with focus on Youth and Students, rooted in Schools & Colleges to enhance quality of education through arts.
It seeks to inspire youth through experiencing the mysticism embodied in the rich and heterogeneous cultural tapestry of Indian and World Heritage, with a hope that the beauty, grace, values, and wisdom embodied in these arts will influence their way of life and thinking and inspire one to become a better human being.

Events like the In Tune In Touch Premier League strengthened team spirit, while the year culminated in a grand finale, celebrating months of dedication and artistic expression. As it continues to grow, In Tune In Touch remains committed to pushing creative boundaries and creating unforgettable experiences.
The Big Bank Theory is an initiative by the Self-Financed Department of Jai Hind College, dedicated to enhancing financial literacy and providing students with a deeper understanding of Banking and Insurance. Through expert-led sessions, industry visits, and interactive learning experiences, this initiative bridges the gap between academic knowledge and real-world financial applications. By offering hands-on exposure to key industry concepts, The Big Bank Theory equips students with the skills and insights needed to navigate the evolving financial landscape.
Leaders in Impact Finance & ESG (LIFE) at Jai Hind College is a student-led initiative by the Department of BAF, BBI, and BFM, dedicated to integrating profitability with purpose in modern finance. LIFE promotes Impact Finance and ESG Investing through practical applications, including The Impact Auction, where participants analyze companies from an ESG perspective in a dynamic bidding challenge. The initiative also hosts The LIFE Podcast, featuring insights from industry leaders like Ms. Purva Dafle, providing real-world perspectives on sustainable finance. Through interactive events and expert discussions, LIFE at Jai Hind College equips students with the skills to navigate the evolving financial landscape responsibly
The Chemical Society of Jai Hind College offers a vibrant platform for students to explore, learn, and engage with the fascinating world of chemistry. Through initiatives like ChemTalk, students connect with experts and gain insights into contemporary developments in the field. In addition, regular workshops and training sessions on sophisticated analytical instruments and industry relevant skills are organised to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world applications. Beyond academics, the Society actively engages in awareness and outreach programs, emphasizing the role of chemistry in everyday life and its potential to address societal challenges. With a balanced focus on knowledge sharing, skill development, and social responsibility, the Chemical Society strives to inspire a new generation of chemists equipped to make meaningful contributions to science and society.
