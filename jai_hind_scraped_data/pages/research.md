TITLE: Research | Jai Hind College

The Central Instrumentation Facility (CIF) at Jai Hind College, inaugurated on 14th July 2015, is a state-of-the-art laboratory housing sophisticated analytical instruments designed to support advanced scientific research. These instruments have been procured through generous funding from DST-FIST, UGC, and the college management. The facility is accessible to faculty and students of the college and is also extended to external researchers. It serves as a hub for student and faculty research, while also hosting hands-on training workshops and internship programs, fostering skill development and innovation.
