TITLE: Junior College | Jai Hind College

The Junior College section of is a dynamic multidisciplinary institution offering quality education in the fields of Science, Commerce, and Arts. Aided by the Government of Maharashtra, the college has consistently nurtured academic excellence and holistic development among students.
Over the years, the institution has built a strong legacy of outstanding performance in board examinations, with students progressing to successful careers in medicine, engineering, business, journalism, and several other professional fields. The Junior College is guided by a dedicated and experienced faculty that fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and personal growth. Through continuous mentorship and meaningful student-teacher interaction, the college creates a supportive environment where young minds are encouraged to achieve their fullest potential.
A student enters the college after passing the std X exams and is considered a student of junior college for 2 years. 
A student studies 8 subjects and appears for 4 exams through the year in Std XI. On successful completion of the exams the student is promoted to std XII. In Std XII a student again studies 8 subjects and at the end of the year appears for a board exam conducted by the state of Maharashtra.
