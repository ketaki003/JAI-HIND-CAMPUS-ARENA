JAI HIND COLLEGE - AKRUTI KENDRA
Advanced Knowledge for Rural and Urban Technology Implementation 
Deployment of DAE-BARC Technologies for Societal Benefit

❑ The AKRUTI Programme showcases the practical value of BARC 
technologies for rural and semi-urban communities, delivering societal 
benefits at affordable costs. 
❑ The programme nurtures techno-preneurship, enabling ventures based on 
200+ DAE-BARC technologies and helps create livelihoods within villages 
themselves.
Now recognised as an official AKRUTI Kendra, Jai Hind College, Empowered 
Autonomous serves as a hub to encourage entrepreneurship among 
students, visitors, and skilled youth. The Kendra spreads awareness of 
available facilities and provides hands-on opportunities to observe, 
evaluate, and experiment with BARC technologies.
❑ For budding entrepreneurs, the Kendra becomes the first point of contact 
with DAE-BARC, guiding them in registering firms and applying for 
commercialization licenses to bring BARC technologies to market. 
❑ We aim to incubate ideas, mentor startups, and help innovators turn 
research into reality. Whether you are a student, a working professional, 
or someone looking to begin a new venture, this is a space to learn, 
innovate, and grow.
